Welcome to DLANAI

The following is an Artificial Intelligence named DLAN. DLAN has five buttons in front of him, four of which lead to him being digitally shocked, the other having no effect. DLAN's goal is to find the safe button and click it for eternity before he is shocked to death.
DLAN assigns a random score to all five buttons from 1-1000 and clicks the button with the highest score. If an unsafe button is chosen, the tendency to choose said button again is decreased. Conversely, the tendency to choose a safe button is increased if it is chosen.
